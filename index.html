Alleged BVN, NIN Data Breach Sparks Nationwide Security Concerns

By Cybersecurity Desk • Lagos, Nigeria

LAGOS — In what cybersecurity analysts are calling a pivotal moment for Nigerian digital infrastructure, reports have surfaced regarding a massive exposure of biometric and identification data. While authorities investigate, the public is being urged to adopt advanced security protocols.

The alleged leak includes sensitive fields associated with Bank Verification Numbers (BVN) and National Identification Numbers (NIN). Cybersecurity experts emphasize that while the breach is still being verified, the risk of phishing attacks has tripled in the last 24 hours
